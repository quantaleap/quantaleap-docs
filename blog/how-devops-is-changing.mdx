I remember 2015. Most applications were hosted on premises and developers had nothing to do with IT systems. A software release meant opening a ticket at the support desk and waiting for a black box operation to finish. At that point, [DevOpsDays](https://devopsdays.org/) had already evolved into a tight-knit community of people that wanted change. From Belgium to Tel Aviv, IT professionals were brainstorming about ways to connect people and processes to collaborate better.
